# Code of Conduct

Vuoom follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

The short version:

- **Be kind and constructive.** Critique code and ideas, never people.
- **Be welcoming.** Newcomers, non-native English speakers, and first-time
  contributors are explicitly welcome here.
- **No harassment of any kind** — in issues, PRs, discussions, or anywhere else
  connected to this project.

Maintainers may edit, lock, or remove contributions that violate this, and ban
repeat offenders.

**Reporting:** email `anisnur19315@gmail.com` or contact
[@Razee4315](https://github.com/Razee4315) directly. Reports are handled
confidentially.
